Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Introduction

BagreType is an academic research project developed by students at Universidade de Franca (UniFran) to analyze and optimize keyboard layouts using machine learning.

Data We Collect

  • Typing Data: Response times, keystroke sequences, and typing patterns for research purposes
  • Account Information: Email address and authentication credentials (stored securely via Supabase)
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, and general usage statistics

How We Use Your Data

  • To conduct academic research on keyboard layout optimization
  • To provide personalized typing lessons and practice sessions
  • To improve the effectiveness of the application
  • To analyze typing performance and generate reports

Data Security

We use Supabase for secure authentication and data storage. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share your personal information with third parties.

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data deletion
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of data collection (though this may limit functionality)

Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us at: privacy@bagretype.com
