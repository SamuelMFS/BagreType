Security Policy

Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in BagreType, please contact us at: security@bagretype.com

We take security seriously and will respond to all reports as quickly as possible.

Scope

This policy applies to the BagreType web application and related services.

Supported Versions

Only the latest version of the application receives security updates.

Our Commitment

  • We will respond to your report within 48 hours
  • We will keep you informed of the progress towards resolving the issue
  • We will credit you for the discovery (if you wish)
  • We will not take legal action against you if you act in good faith
